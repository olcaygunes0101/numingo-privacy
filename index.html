Privacy Policy for Numingo

Last updated: October 25, 2025

Introduction

Numingo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Numingo is a simple number guessing game that operates entirely offline on your device. All game statistics and progress are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Information Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game statistics (games played, win rate, average attempts)
  • Game history and scores
  • App preferences and settings

This information never leaves your device and is only accessible by you through the app.

Third-Party Services

Numingo does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app operates completely independently and offline.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns for children using our app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
